## Subject and location (as stated on the source page)

- **Depicted taxon:** *Caligo* sp. (owl butterfly; Nymphalidae).
- **Life status:** wild, at night.
- **Location:** in the vicinity of Tambopata Research Centre, Tambopata
  National Reserve, Madre de Dios region, Peru.
- **Date taken:** 2023-07-28 07:40:22 (local time as stated in EXIF).

The bright round feature near the middle of the frame is the butterfly's
**compound eye**, not a wing eyespot. Public copy must not describe or crop
it as a wing eyespot.

## Changes made to the original

- Colour space normalised to sRGB.
- All metadata stripped (`-strip`) except intrinsic pixel data.
- Widescreen files: proportional downscale only; no crop.
- Portrait files: horizontal crop of `2000 × 2142` pixels at offset `+50 +0`
  (drops the mostly-empty right-hand black field so the head, compound eye,
  antenna base, and left-wing scale texture remain visible on narrow
  viewports). Proportional downscale after crop.
- JPEG re-encoded at quality 82 (widescreen and portrait) / 78 (poster),
  progressive, 4:2:0 chroma subsampling.
- Not mirrored. Not modified to suggest the compound eye is a wing eyespot.
  No synthetic wings, gradients, or overlays added.
